ABOUT

The MSU Roller Hockey program is known as one of the most respected and elite programs in the country. The team is consistently ranked among the top ten teams in the National Collegiate Roller Hockey Association. The success MSU Roller Hockey has enjoyed comes from its unmatched determination to work hard and exceed expectations.
